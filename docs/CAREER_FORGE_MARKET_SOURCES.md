## Negative-findings protocol

Statements such as “no public evidence found” mean the audited pages did not demonstrate the behavior. They do **not** assert that the capability is absent from account-only, enterprise, experimental, or later product versions. ResumeForge and Bragora were treated as direct moat risks precisely because their public claims overlap strongly even though their detailed workflow is not observable without an account.
