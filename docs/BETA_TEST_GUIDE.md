# Career Forge — Beta Test Guide

**Release:** `v0.10.0-beta.1` · **Live:** https://career-forge-lite.vercel.app · **No account needed** (everything is stored on your device).

You are one of the first outside testers. The goal is to see whether Career Forge makes one real job-search task **easier than your usual process** — and to tell us where it almost lost you.

## Before you start
- Use a real (or realistic) résumé or work history. Career Forge never invents facts — it only uses what you approve.
- It's a **public beta**: every generated résumé is a *draft*. Review every claim, date, and heading before you send anything to an employer.
- Your career data stays in your browser. Clearing site data or switching devices/browsers starts you over.

## Pick ONE path and finish it

### 1. Update My Résumé (you already have one)
1. On the home screen choose **Update My Résumé**.
2. Paste your existing résumé text (or upload the file) and click **Extract proposed evidence**.
3. **Approve** the facts that are true (use "Approve section" to go faster), then **Finish review**.
4. You'll immediately see **"Your first résumé bullets — ready now"** — a preview cleaned from what you approved. *This is the early payoff; nothing is invented.*
5. Choose a job direction → **Forge complete résumé pack** → open **My Résumé** to view/edit/export.

### 2. Build My First Résumé (little or no résumé yet)
1. Choose **Build My First Résumé**.
2. Add **one** recent role or project with a couple of specific things you did. That's enough to start — skip the rest.
3. Approve it, see your first cleaned bullets, then pick a direction and forge the pack.

### 3. Practice Interview
1. Choose **Practice Interview**.
2. Pick the round you're preparing for. You'll get practice questions and an answer framework **immediately**, even with no profile.

## After you have a finished résumé
A short **"Help shape the beta"** card appears. Please answer it — it's the whole point of the beta:
- Was this **easier than your usual process**?
- **Would you use this** for your next application?
- **What almost stopped you?** (be blunt — a confusing step, a missing option, a moment you nearly gave up)
- Optional: a sentence we could quote.

It saves on your device only; nothing is sent automatically.

## Things we already know (please still tell us if they bite)
- **Suggested job directions can be off** — e.g. an operations résumé may be offered support/tech lanes. You can still pick any direction and finish; tell us if the suggestions felt wrong.
- **Exports** are PDF / DOCX / a full ZIP. Open what you download and check it reads correctly.
- Payments are **off** during this beta — nothing will ever charge you.

## What we most want to hear
1. Did you reach a **finished, sendable artifact**? How long did it take?
2. Where did you get **confused or stuck**?
3. Would you **use it again** — and would you tell a friend?

Reply with your answers (and the optional testimonial) however you received this invite. Thank you — you're shaping what ships next.
