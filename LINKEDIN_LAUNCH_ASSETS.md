## Concise Launch Post Draft

I shipped Career Forge Lite, a free Product Lab module from Koinophobia Labs.

It helps early-career candidates turn everyday work experience into recruiter-ready resume language through a guided interview.

The tool collects role target, role family, tools, responsibilities, scope, and outcomes, then generates an editable ATS-safe resume draft plus a concise LinkedIn headline.

No login. No fake ATS score. No job placement promises. Just a cleaner way to translate real work into stronger career language.

Try it here: https://career-forge-lite.vercel.app

GitHub: https://github.com/koinophobia-labs/career-forge-lite

## Technical GitHub-Focused Post Draft

I pushed Career Forge Lite as an open MVP for Koinophobia Labs.

Stack:
- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- App Router
- Local state only

The interesting part is the deterministic resume generator and role-intelligence layer. Users can pick from mapped career targets, confirm a role family, select role-aware responsibility chips, add scope/outcome signals, then edit the generated resume and LinkedIn text before copying or printing.

I kept the scope intentionally tight: no auth, no database, no payments, no job board, and no fake ATS score.

Repo: https://github.com/koinophobia-labs/career-forge-lite
Live: https://career-forge-lite.vercel.app
